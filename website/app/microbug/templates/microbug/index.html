BBC Bug

    BBC Bug is a brand new programmable device that gets you writing computer code. Simply write your code or edit other peoples and download it to your Bug. Watch the LEDs light up and your Bug come to life.

    Now you can start getting really creative, share code and amaze your friends. You can show off everything you create!
